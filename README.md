PRUMO

Rules:
A player will place a card which shares a factor with the card on the stack.
When the player has one card left, they can yell PRUMO, same rules as UNO, so if you forget opponents can call like in UNO

Regular Numbers:
2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 12, 14, 15, 16, 18, 21, 25

General Specials:
Swap: Two players swap hands
Rotate: Each player passes their hand to the left
Reverse: Reverses order of turns
Block: Blocks the next player

Maths Specials:
Prime: Next player must play a prime, or draw the number of cards below
Square: Next player must play a square, or draw the number of cards below
mod n: Next player must play a card which is divisible by n, or draw number of cards below
+2-9 cards: however a player can call challenge whenever they are played.

Maths Big Specials:
Infinite Descent: All players must play cards in decreasing order until a player fails to do so. That player must draw cards equivalent to the top card on the stack.
Sieve of Eratosthenes: Player must call out a prime factor, then all players are allowed to discard any number of cards onto the stack which are multiples of that prime factor 
Collatz: Starting number before Collatz, then people race to play the next card in the sequence. If someone plays a Collatz with no immediate successor, they draw 2.

Maths Passives:
Induction: If you play n, you can immediately play n+1
Exhaustion: If a player holds every single number available exactly once, they win the game.
